About E-Government Policy

Our government provides digital solutions for identity management, taxation, healthcare, and certification services. Citizens can easily access and manage their official documents online. The Data Privacy Policy outlines the guidelines for handling personal and sensitive information securely. It applies to all employees and third-party vendors who process user data and aims to ensure compliance with regulations like GDPR and CCPA. Under this policy, all personal data must be encrypted, securely stored, and accessed only by authorized personnel. Employees must not share or misuse personal information. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action or legal consequences. This policy is reviewed annually to maintain its effectiveness and relevance.
